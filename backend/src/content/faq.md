# Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to common questions about working with Cloud Miami.

## General Questions

### What does "AI-Native" mean?
AI-Native means we build AI into everything we do. From chatbots to content optimization to automation, we leverage artificial intelligence to improve results and efficiency.

### Do you work with businesses outside Miami?
While we're based in Miami and specialize in helping local businesses, we work with clients nationwide. Technology allows us to serve clients anywhere.

### What industries do you work with?
We work with B2B businesses across various industries including:
- Technology and SaaS companies
- Professional services (law firms, accountants, consultants)
- Healthcare and medical practices
- Real estate and construction
- Financial services
- E-commerce and retail
- Manufacturing and logistics

### How long have you been in business?
Cloud Miami was founded to help B2B businesses leverage modern technology. Our team has decades of combined experience in web development, marketing, and AI.

## Services Questions

### What's included in a website project?
Every website project includes planning, design, development, testing, launch, and 30 days of support. We also provide training so you can manage your own content.

### How long does it take to build a website?
Simple websites take 2-4 weeks. Complex web applications take 6-12 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline in our proposal.

### Do you offer ongoing maintenance?
Yes! We offer monthly maintenance packages that include updates, security monitoring, and technical support.

### Can you help with existing websites?
Absolutely. We can audit, optimize, redesign, or add features to existing websites.

## Process Questions

### What happens during the discovery call?
The discovery call is a free 30-minute conversation where we learn about your business, goals, and challenges. There's no obligation - it's just a chance to see if we're a good fit.

### How do you handle revisions?
We include revision rounds in our proposals. We want to make sure you're happy with the final result.

### Do you provide hosting?
Yes, we can handle hosting as part of our services. We use reliable cloud infrastructure with excellent performance.

### What happens after launch?
We provide 30 days of free support after launch. We also offer ongoing maintenance packages for clients who want continued assistance.

## AI and Technology Questions

### Is my business ready for AI?
Most businesses can benefit from AI today, even in simple ways like chatbots or content optimization. We start small and scale based on your needs.

### Is AI expensive?
AI tools have become very affordable. Many AI solutions cost less than traditional alternatives while delivering better results.

### Will AI replace humans at my business?
No, AI is designed to augment human capabilities, not replace them. It handles repetitive tasks so your team can focus on high-value work.
